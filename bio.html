    My Life

  • I was born in Dallas, Texas on September 28 1990.
  • I then moved to Chicago and New York before reaching Wellesley, Massachusetts, my current home.
  • I went to Wellesley Public Schools until high school graduation. I participated in Key Club and practiced Sailing, Track, and Fencing.
  • I went on to attend Tufts University where I am majoring in International Literary and Visual Studies with a focus in Japanese and English Literature and Sequential Art Narrative.
  • I am also minoring in Computer Science.
  • I am an officer in the Tufts chapter of Students for Sensible Drug Policy and a board member of the Tufts Mountain Club.
  • I enjoying writing, reading, and drawing as well as biking and hiking on a beautiful day.
